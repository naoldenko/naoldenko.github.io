Are You Tired?

Are you tired?

From the constant chatting,

The constant show & tell,

The constant yearning

For more but constantly received

Nothing back.

Heart looking for heart

But instead, you look toward art.

To evoke your confined emotions,

In a world filled with discussions

About the opposing participants,

Who were once infants;

A same start, but a different end.

In a cycle, we must partake

For survival's sake

Will you make a mistake?

Will you not break?

Do you feel tired?

Maybe so

But,

I try to flow.
