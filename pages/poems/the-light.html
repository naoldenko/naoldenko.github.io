Naol Denko — Poems

The Light

The more I recognize

The more lonely I feel…

Always being in the middle

Never hot nor cold

Always feeling lukewarm

Like I'm room temp water

It's like why bother?

But there's something that urges

For me to keep running

And to keep hoping

By looking towards the "Light"

The "Light" will lead the way

For one day I'll go away

And no longer stay

Hurray?
